Wearable Tech for Athletes: Monitoring Performance and Health in Real-Time Sports Venue Technology
Abstract We can see with wearable technology in sports becoming more popular it has many benefits. Devices such as smartwatches, fitness trackers, and even sport sensors are utilized in everyday practice and games for athletes. It gives real time data to coaches and sports professionals, showcasing how hard an individual is training, but also monitoring their health. Making sure that the biometric sensors are giving signs for heart rate, oxygen saturation levels, and even sleep patterns. This plays a big role in injury prevention towards athletes, making sure they stay on top of their game during practice and competition. Wearable tech is one of the most important tools in sports across the world with most athletes using them to monitor their health and to reach new heights.

A Review of the Physical, Societal and Ecenomic Effects of Wearable Devices in Sports The Sport Journal
Abstract This article brings extensive research towards wearable devices in sports, stating how in sports it can help with injuries, data analysis on performance, and to monitor physical health of athletes in the sports world. But sport wearables are not only for athletes, but they can be useful towards an average or beginner user. It can be a highly effective tool towards overall physical health making sure you are happy and healthy everyday. As well as mitigating injuries. Not only can it help track sleep and your oxygen levels, but also your emotional state and how much you are improving towards your goals. Some people may think of wearable devices as an issue as if they do not trust it or it can harm athletes upon collision if worn a certain way. But overall, the technology itself evaluates effects on physical and mental well being, making it an important step towards sports and athletes.
